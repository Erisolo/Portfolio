PATREM OMNIPOTENTEM

Chronicle of a Dying God

★ Best atmosphere winner in the 2026 ConJamOn Jam, sponsored by EA. ★

In this horror simmulation narrative game, you're a pariah in a hostile world, ruled by an equally ruthless and agonizing super computer.

You can explore this empty world, full only of what will soon become the Father's corpse, and interact with his inhabitants. But be careful. They don't take kindly to strangers, and the Father is ever vigilant.

My contributions as Main Creative Director:

★ Design
- Concept art for all characters.
- Map and level design for all rooms.
- Game design for the combat mechanics and world.
- Atmosphere design and coordination.
★ Writting
- Script for all the characters, in and out of battle.
★ Support programming
